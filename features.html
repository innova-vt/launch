Appointment Scheduling

  • Streamlined scheduling for appointments
  • Automated reminders for clients and staff
  • Customizable time slots and availability
  • Efficiently manage appointments and boarding to optimize your practice's workflow

Client & Patient Management

  • Centralized database for client information
  • Detailed patient profiles, including medical history
  • Appointment history and upcoming visits
  • Communication logs
  • Simplify the organization of client details and patient records for enhanced service delivery

Medical Records

  • Visible and thorough SOAP layout
  • Secure and accessible electronic medical records
  • Patient treatment history and diagnoses
  • Quick retrieval for efficient decision-making
  • Maintain comprehensive electronic medical records to facilitate informed decision-making and continuity of pet care

SOAP Layout

  • Standardize patient documentation using the SOAP format for structured and organized recording.

Billing and Invoicing

  • Flexible billing options for services and products
  • Automated invoicing and payment
  • Integration with payment gateways
  • Expense tracking and P&L financial reporting

Accounting (Billing, Invoicing, Profit and Loss)

  • Handle financial transactions seamlessly, including billing, invoicing, and profit/loss analysis

Staff (Payroll, Availability)

  • Manage staff-related aspects such as payroll processing and availability.

Security (Permissions, Roles, Practice Logs)

  • Ensure data security and access control through customizable permissions, roles, and practice logs

Laboratory

  • Manage test orders and results records, ensuring optimal patient care and practice workflow

Stock and Inventory Management

  • Comprehensive inventory history
  • Product catalog with details and pricing
  • Medicine dispense and restock
  • Efficiently manage stock and inventory levels, minimizing stockouts and optimizing supply chain operations

Payment Gateway

  • Integrates with payment processors to accept various forms of payment, including credit/debit cards and electronic fund transfers

Client Portal

  • Provide clients with a convenient and secure online platform to access pet information, schedule appointments, and communicate with the practice

Reporting

  • Financial reports for revenue analysis
  • Diagnosis reports for trends
  • Generate reports to analyze clinic performance, practice trends, and operational efficiency.

Client Communication (Messaging, Email Notifications)

  • Engage with clients effectively through messaging and email notifications for appointment reminders and updates

Task Creation

Customization

  • Dedicated support and training resources
