Features

SmartDVM offers a comprehensive suite of features to elevate your veterinary practice

Appointment Scheduling

  • Streamlined scheduling for appointments
  • Automated reminders for clients and staff
  • Customizable time slots and availability

Client & Patient Management

  • Centralized database for client information
  • Detailed patient profiles, including medical history
  • Appointment history and upcoming visits
  • Communication logs

Medical Records

  • Visible and thorough SOAP layout
  • Secure and accessible electronic medical records
  • Patient treatment history and diagnoses
  • Quick retrieval for efficient decision-making

Billing and Invoicing

  • Flexible billing options for services and products
  • Automated invoicing and payment
  • Integration with payment gateways
  • Expense tracking and P&L financial reporting

Stock and Inventory Management

  • Comprehensive inventory history
  • Product catalog with details and pricing
  • Medicine dispense and restock

Reporting

  • Reports for various aspects of the clinic
  • Financial reports for revenue analysis
  • Diagnosis reports for trends

Client Communication

  • Client portal area
  • Secure notification system for client interaction
  • Appointment confirmations and updates

Tasks

  • Create and assign routine tasks
  • Automatic reminders for follow-ups
  • Integration with staff calendars

Payroll Management

  • Employee profiles with role-specific access
  • Pay slip and leave management

Customization

  • Customization options based on user feedback and requests
  • Dedicated support and training resources
